HERITAGE

Over a Century of Innovation

Founded in 1909 by August Horch, Audi has been at the forefront of automotive engineering for over 110 years. The name "Audi" comes from the Latin translation of the founder's surname "Horch" – meaning "listen" in German.

From the revolutionary quattro all-wheel drive system to today's fully electric e-tron models, we've consistently pushed the boundaries of what's possible.

Milestones

Key moments in Audi's history that shaped the automotive world.

1909

Foundation of Audi

August Horch establishes Audi Automobilwerke GmbH in Zwickau, Germany.

1932

Four Rings Born

Audi, DKW, Horch, and Wanderer merge to form Auto Union AG – the four rings are born.

1980

quattro Revolution

Audi presents the first permanent all-wheel drive sports coupe at the Geneva Motor Show.

1994

A8 with ASF

Introduction of the Audi Space Frame (ASF) aluminum body in the A8 flagship sedan.

2018

Electric Era Begins

Launch of the first fully electric Audi e-tron, marking the start of our electric future.
